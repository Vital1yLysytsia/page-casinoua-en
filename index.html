Football betting is a recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

Initially, football pools were one of the forms of entertainment. For example, one can mention pari-mutuel – a long-standing system that originated in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • Outside the stadiums, fans bought tickets at a minimal price (later, tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, where they were cut out);
  • Fans filled in cards with the results of upcoming “game number one” matches, which they believed were the most likely;
  • The coupons were handed over to a pool-collecting agent or sent to the organizing company by mail.

The specialized office guaranteed that within a week there would be at least one winner. The lucky ones were determined by the number of points scored among all fans who had sent in their cards.

The pari-mutuel system allowed people to earn by making the first football bets with large payouts. For instance, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned more than 3 million pounds. He managed to correctly predict 8 matches that ended in a 2:2 draw.

The pool system was successful until “bookmakers” began to appear in the form in which they exist today. Fans of the “game number one” gained the opportunity to place bets with much greater comfort.
